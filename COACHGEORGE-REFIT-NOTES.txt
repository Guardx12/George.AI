CoachGeorge refit notes

This version moves /coachgeorge away from being an exact macro / fixed meal-plan builder and toward a coaching app that sits alongside NutriCheck or MyFitnessPal.

Changed in this ZIP:
- Removed AI wording from the page metadata and visible header.
- Repositioned Coach George as a fitness coach for meals, training, staying on track, and getting back on it when life happens.
- Changed the setup modal from “Set your targets” to “Set up George” / coaching profile.
- Reframed targets as guidance rather than exact source-of-truth food logging.
- Changed “Build / Update Plan” to “Get Today’s Guidance”.
- Added quick coaching prompts: what should I eat tonight, I’m hungry, my weight went up, I messed up.
- Added safer full-reset confirmation so users do not accidentally wipe their profile.
- De-emphasised fixed meal plans, swapping meals, and shopping list controls.
- Updated George’s controlled replies so he directs users to trackers for exact grams and uses George for coaching decisions.

Still to do later:

- Add proper phases: fat loss, boxing, strength, maintenance, running, reset etc.
- Add editable dashboard/settings for profile and active phase.
- Add daily/weekly check-ins and progress logs.
- Add proper proactive coaching summaries instead of full transcript storage.
